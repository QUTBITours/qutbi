Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

+

Our office is open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. However, our WhatsApp support is available 24/7 for urgent inquiries.

Do I need to make an appointment before visiting?

+

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment for personalized attention, especially for complex travel plans or visa consultations. This ensures that our travel experts are available to assist you properly.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

+

We typically respond to email inquiries within 24 hours. For faster response, you can reach us via phone or WhatsApp. Urgent inquiries are prioritized, especially for last-minute travel plans or visa emergencies.

Can you customize packages according to my budget?

+

Yes, we specialize in creating customized travel packages based on your preferences, group size, and budget. Contact us with your requirements, and our team will craft a personalized itinerary for you.
