Experience the glamour, luxury, and cultural richness of Dubai with our carefully crafted 5-day package. From the magnificent Burj Khalifa to the traditional desert safari, this tour combines the ultra-modern cityscape with authentic Arabian experiences.

Dubai, a city of superlatives, offers visitors a glimpse into both the ancient traditions of the Emirates and its ambitious vision for the future. Our package ensures you experience the best of both worlds.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai

Meals: None
  • Arrive at Dubai International Airport
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Transfer to your hotel
  • Check-in and relax
  • Evening free for leisure activities
  • Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 2: Dubai City Tour & Dhow Cruise

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Half-day Dubai city tour including:
  • Visit to Jumeirah Mosque (exterior view)
  • Photo stop at Burj Al Arab
  • Visit to Jumeirah Beach
  • Drive through Sheikh Zayed Road
  • Visit to Dubai Museum and Al Fahidi Fort
  • Drive through the spice and gold souks
  • Return to hotel for afternoon rest
  • Evening Dhow Cruise with dinner
  • Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 3: Desert Safari

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Morning free for leisure or shopping
  • Afternoon Desert Safari tour including:
  • Dune bashing in 4x4 vehicles
  • Camel riding
  • Sand boarding (subject to weather conditions)
  • Henna painting
  • Arabic costumes for photographs
  • Shisha smoking
  • BBQ dinner
  • Belly dance show (except during religious holidays)
  • Tanoura dance show
  • Return to hotel
  • Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 4: Burj Khalifa & Dubai Mall

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Visit to Burj Khalifa (124th floor)
  • Time to explore Dubai Mall
  • Watch the spectacular Dubai Fountain show
  • Rest of the day free for shopping or leisure
  • Optional visit to Wild Wadi Water Park (at additional cost)
  • Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 5: Departure

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Check-out from the hotel (standard check-out time is 12:00 PM)
  • Transfer to Dubai International Airport for your departure flight
  • End of tour services
