Package Highlights

  • Kochi city tour including Fort Kochi and Marine Drive
  • Munnar hill station with tea garden visits and waterfalls
  • Thekkady wildlife sanctuary and spice plantation tour
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey backwaters
  • 5 nights accommodation in 3-star hotels/houseboat

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala with this 6-day journey covering hill stations, wildlife, and the famed backwaters. Perfect for nature lovers, families, and honeymooners seeking a peaceful getaway.

Enjoy traditional Kerala hospitality, stunning landscapes, and cultural richness from coast to hills.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

Meals: Dinner
  • Arrival at Kochi Airport
  • Transfer to hotel and check-in
  • Evening at Marine Drive

Day 2: Kochi to Munnar

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Drive to Munnar (approx. 4 hours)
  • Visit Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls en route
  • Check-in and relax at hotel

Day 3: Munnar Sightseeing

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Visit Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point
  • Explore tea gardens and local markets

Day 4: Munnar to Thekkady

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Drive to Thekkady (approx. 3.5 hours)
  • Spice plantation tour and wildlife sanctuary visit
  • Optional Kathakali or Kalaripayattu show

Day 5: Thekkady to Alleppey

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Drive to Alleppey (approx. 4 hours)
  • Check-in to houseboat and cruise the backwaters
  • Enjoy lunch (optional), evening tea, and sunset views

Day 6: Departure

Meals: Breakfast
  • Check-out and transfer to Kochi Airport
  • Departure with beautiful memories of Kerala
