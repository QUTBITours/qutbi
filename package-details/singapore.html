Singapore Delight

Duration 5 Days / 4 Nights
Price ₹52,000 per person
Best Time February - April

Package Highlights

  • Night Safari
  • City Tour of Singapore
  • Sentosa Island with cable car & Wings of Time
  • Universal Studios full-day access
  • Gardens by the Bay & Marina Bay Sands visit
  • 4 nights stay in a 3-star hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Airport transfers
  • All taxes included

Discover the vibrant charm of Singapore, where modern innovation meets cultural heritage. Our 5-day tour brings you to iconic sights like Marina Bay Sands, Sentosa Island, and Universal Studios for a perfect blend of entertainment and exploration.

Singapore’s clean streets, stunning architecture, and lush gardens make it an unforgettable destination. Let us take care of everything while you experience the Lion City.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Singapore

Meals: None
  • Arrive at Changi International Airport
  • Meet our representative and transfer to hotel
  • Check-in and relax
  • Evening free for leisure
  • Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 2: Singapore City Tour & Night Safari

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Half-day city tour including Merlion Park, Chinatown, Little India & Orchard Road
  • Afternoon at leisure
  • Evening Night Safari with tram ride and animal shows
  • Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 3: Sentosa Island Tour

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit to Sentosa via cable car
  • Trick Eye Museum, Madame Tussauds (optional)
  • Wings of Time evening light & water show
  • Return and overnight stay at hotel

Day 4: Universal Studios Singapore

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Full-day pass to Universal Studios
  • Experience themed rides, shows, and attractions
  • Evening return to hotel
  • Overnight stay

Day 5: Gardens by the Bay & Departure

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Gardens by the Bay & Cloud Forest Dome
  • Check-out and transfer to airport
  • End of tour
