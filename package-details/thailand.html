Thailand Explorer

Duration 7 Days / 6 Nights
Price ₹38,000 per person
Best Time November - April

Package Highlights

  • Bangkok City Tour
  • Pattaya Coral Island Tour
  • Alcazar Cabaret Show
  • Floating Market Visit
  • Safari World & Marine Park
  • 6 nights accommodation in 3-star hotels
  • Daily breakfast
  • Airport transfers
  • All taxes included

Unwind in Thailand's tropical paradise with this 7-day trip covering both urban wonders and beach bliss. From exploring the vibrant city of Bangkok to the sunny shores of Pattaya, this package brings you the perfect blend of culture, entertainment, and relaxation.

Enjoy crystal-clear beaches, lively markets, delicious Thai cuisine, and warm hospitality while visiting some of the country's top tourist spots.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok – Transfer to Pattaya

Meals: None
  • Arrive at Bangkok Airport
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Transfer to Pattaya (approx. 2 hours)
  • Check-in at the hotel and relax
  • Evening free for leisure

Day 2: Coral Island Tour

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Speed boat to Coral Island
  • Time for swimming, snorkeling, and beach fun
  • Thai lunch on the island
  • Return to hotel and rest
  • Optional: Alcazar Show in evening (extra cost)

Day 3: Pattaya – Transfer to Bangkok

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast and check-out
  • Drive to Bangkok
  • Check-in at hotel in Bangkok
  • Evening visit to local markets or shopping malls

Day 4: Bangkok City Tour

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Visit to Golden Buddha & Marble Temple
  • Drive through Chinatown and city landmarks
  • Shopping time at MBK or Siam Paragon Mall

Day 5: Safari World & Marine Park

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Safari World & Marine Park
  • Watch animal shows and explore wildlife exhibits
  • Buffet lunch included
  • Return to hotel in evening

Day 6: Floating Market & Leisure

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Morning visit to Damnoen Saduak Floating Market
  • Free time for shopping or optional massage/spa

Day 7: Departure

Meals: Breakfast
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Check-out and transfer to Bangkok Airport
  • End of tour services

Package Includes

  • 6 nights in 3-star hotel (Pattaya & Bangkok)
  • Daily breakfast
  • Coral Island tour with lunch
  • Bangkok city tour
  • Safari World with lunch
  • Airport and intercity transfers
  • All applicable taxes

Package Excludes

  • Airfare and visa fees
  • Travel insurance
  • Meals not mentioned above
  • Optional tours and activities
  • Personal expenses
  • Tips and gratuities
