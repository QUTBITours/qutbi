Kashmir Delight

Duration 6 Days / 5 Nights
Price ₹22,000 per person
Best Time March - October
Book This Package

Package Highlights

  • Srinagar city tour with Dal Lake Shikara ride
  • Day trip to Gulmarg - cable car ride (optional)
  • Pahalgam sightseeing including Aru & Betaab Valley
  • Sonmarg excursion with snow activities (seasonal)
  • 5 nights accommodation in 3-star hotels
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • All sightseeing transfers in private vehicle
  • All applicable taxes

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir with this 6-day journey through valleys, meadows, and snow-capped peaks. Relax on a Shikara boat, explore iconic mountain towns, and immerse yourself in the culture and cuisine of India's crown jewel.

Ideal for couples, families, and nature lovers looking for a mix of leisure and adventure in one of the most scenic destinations of India.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Meals: Dinner
  • Arrive at Srinagar Airport
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Transfer to hotel / houseboat
  • Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake

Day 2: Gulmarg Day Trip

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Drive to Gulmarg (approx. 2 hours)
  • Explore meadows and gondola ride (optional)
  • Return to Srinagar in evening

Day 3: Srinagar Sightseeing

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Visit Mughal Gardens – Nishat, Shalimar, and Chashme Shahi
  • Explore old Srinagar markets
  • Optional visit to Hazratbal Shrine

Day 4: Pahalgam Excursion

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Drive to Pahalgam (approx. 2.5 hours)
  • Visit Aru Valley and Betaab Valley
  • Enjoy river-side views and local shopping
  • Return to Srinagar

Day 5: Sonmarg Excursion

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Day trip to Sonmarg (approx. 2.5 hours)
  • Explore glaciers and optional snow activities
  • Return to Srinagar in evening

Day 6: Departure

Meals: Breakfast
  • Check-out from hotel
  • Transfer to Srinagar Airport for return journey

Package Includes

  • 5 nights accommodation in Srinagar
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • All transfers and sightseeing in private vehicle
  • Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • All tolls, taxes, parking, and driver allowance

Package Excludes

  • Airfare or train tickets
  • Lunch and beverages
  • Any gondola or pony rides
  • Personal expenses and tips
  • Travel insurance
