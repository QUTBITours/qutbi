Canada Visa Requirements

Processing Time 7–15 working days
Validity Up to 10 years
Interview Required No

Types of Canada Visas

Visitor Visa (Temporary Resident Visa)

For tourism, visiting family/friends, or short-term business visits.

Single or multiple entry, valid for up to 10 years, allows up to 6 months stay per visit.

Super Visa

For parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens and permanent residents.

Valid for up to 10 years; allows multiple entries, with a stay of up to 2 years per visit.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid for at least 6 months from your intended date of arrival in Canada with at least 2 blank pages.

Photograph

Two recent passport-sized photographs meeting Canadian visa requirements (35x45mm, white background).

Flight Tickets

Confirmed return flight bookings.

Hotel Booking

Proof of accommodation during stay in Canada.

Bank Statement

Last 3 months bank statement showing sufficient funds for your trip.

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Choose Visa Type

Select the type of visa (Visitor Visa or Super Visa) based on your purpose of visit.

Step 2

Submit Documents

Gather and submit the required documents online or through our agency.

Step 3

Wait for Approval

Visa processing time typically takes 7–15 working days. You’ll receive updates via email.

Step 4

Receive Your Visa

Once approved, collect your visa or eTA and prepare for your travel to Canada.

Important Guidelines

Entry Conditions

Ensure all travel documents align with your visa type and follow Canadian entry conditions.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is strongly recommended to cover health and emergency expenses.

Overstay Penalties

Overstaying your visa can result in fines, deportation, or future travel bans to Canada.

Visa Validity

Ensure your visa is valid for your entire trip duration and check the allowed length of stay.
