Dubai (UAE) Visa Requirements

Processing Time 3-5 working days
Validity 30 days / 90 days
Embassy Fee Starts from ₹5,500

Types of Dubai Visas

Tourist Visa (30 Days)

Fee: ₹7,500

Single entry visa valid for 30 days from the date of entry.

Ideal for short vacations and leisure travel.

Tourist Visa (90 Days)

Fee: ₹14,500

Multiple entry visa valid for 90 days from the date of entry.

Perfect for longer stays and multiple visits.

Transit Visa (96 Hours)

Fee: ₹6,500

Valid for up to 96 hours during transit.

For travelers with a connecting flight through Dubai.

Visit Visa (30 Days)

Fee: ₹7,500

For those visiting family or friends in UAE.

Requires invitation letter from UAE resident.

Required Documents

Passport

Original passport with minimum 6 months validity from the date of travel.

Must have at least 2 blank pages for visa stamping.

Photographs

2 recent color photographs with white background.

Size: 4.3 cm × 5.5 cm (UAE standard size).

Flight Tickets

Confirmed return flight tickets showing entry and exit dates.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed hotel reservation for the entire duration of stay.

Bank Statement

Last 6 months bank statements showing sufficient funds.

Must be stamped and signed by the bank.

Visa Application Form

Completely filled visa application form.

Must be signed by the applicant.

Additional Requirements

For Employed Individuals

  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from employer
  • Employment verification letter stating position and salary
  • Last 3 months salary slips

For Self-Employed Individuals

  • Business registration documents
  • Business bank statements for last 6 months
  • Income tax returns for last 2 years

For Students

  • No Objection Certificate from school/college
  • Student ID card copy
  • Proof of enrollment/bonafide certificate

For Minors (Below 18 years)

  • Birth certificate
  • No Objection Certificate signed by both parents
  • Passport copies of both parents

Visa Processing Timeline

Day 1

Application Submission

Submit all required documents and application form.

Day 2

Initial Processing

Application is checked and entered into the system.

Day 3-4

Embassy Review

Application is reviewed by UAE immigration authorities.

Day 5

Visa Issuance

Visa is issued and ready for collection.

Note: Timeline is approximate and may vary based on current processing volumes and individual case requirements.

Important Guidelines

Visa Validity

Dubai tourist visa is valid for 58 days from the date of issue, and you must enter Dubai within this period.

Stay Duration

You can stay for a maximum of 30 or 90 days (depending on visa type) from the date of entry.

Visa Extension

Tourist visas can be extended for an additional 30 days by paying the applicable fee (must be done before visa expiry).

Entry Restrictions

Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your date of entry into UAE.

Travel Insurance

While not mandatory, comprehensive travel insurance is strongly recommended.

Cultural Considerations

Respect local customs and dress modestly, especially in public places and religious sites.
