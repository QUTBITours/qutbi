Malaysia Visa Requirements

Processing Time 2–5 working days
Validity Up to 3 months
Interview Required No

Types of Malaysia Visas

eVisa

For tourism, family visits, or short-term business travel.

Single entry, valid for 3 months; allows 30 days stay.

eNTRI (Electronic Travel Registration & Information)

For Indian passport holders entering Malaysia directly from India.

Single entry, up to 15 days stay, quicker approval.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid for at least 6 months with 2 blank pages.

Photograph

One recent passport-size photograph (white background, 35x45 mm).

Flight Tickets

Confirmed return flight bookings.

Hotel Booking

Proof of accommodation during stay in Malaysia.

Bank Statement

Last 3 months bank statement with sufficient balance.

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Choose Visa Type

Select between eVisa and eNTRI depending on your travel plan and eligibility.

Step 2

Submit Documents

Upload required documents online or submit to us if you're applying through our agency.

Step 3

Wait for Approval

Receive visa approval by email. Processing usually takes 2–5 working days.

Step 4

Print and Travel

Print your approved eVisa or eNTRI and carry it along with your documents while traveling.

Important Guidelines

Entry Conditions

Ensure your travel route complies with the visa type (e.g., eNTRI requires direct entry from India).

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is recommended but not mandatory.

Overstay Penalties

Overstaying your visa in Malaysia can lead to heavy penalties and future bans.

Visa Validity

Ensure your travel falls within the visa’s validity period and allowed duration of stay.
