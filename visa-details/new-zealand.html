New Zealand Visa Requirements

Processing Time 5–10 working days
Validity Up to 9 months
Interview Required No

Types of New Zealand Visas

Visitor Visa

For tourism, visiting family or friends, or short-term study up to 3 months.

Single entry or multiple entries, valid up to 9 months.

Working Holiday Visa

For young people (18–30 years) who want to travel and work in New Zealand for up to 12 months.

Valid for up to 12 months.

Student Visa

For those wishing to study in New Zealand for more than 3 months.

Valid for the duration of your study course.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid for at least 3 months beyond the intended date of arrival in New Zealand.

Photograph

Two recent passport-sized photographs (white background, 35x45mm).

Flight Tickets

Confirmed round-trip flight booking.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed accommodation for the duration of your stay in New Zealand.

Bank Statement

Last 3 months bank statement showing sufficient funds for the duration of your stay.

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Choose Visa Type

Select the appropriate visa based on your purpose of visit (Visitor, Working Holiday, Study).

Step 2

Submit Documents

Submit the required documents either online or through our agency for processing.

Step 3

Wait for Approval

Visa approval generally takes 5-10 working days. You will be notified once approved.

Step 4

Receive Your Visa

Once your visa is approved, collect it or receive your eVisa and prepare for your travel.

Important Guidelines

Entry Conditions

Ensure you meet all the entry requirements, such as having enough funds for your stay.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is recommended, especially for healthcare coverage during your stay in New Zealand.

Overstay Penalties

Overstaying your visa can result in deportation and future visa application refusals.

Visa Validity

Ensure that your visa remains valid throughout your stay and that you leave New Zealand before it expires.
