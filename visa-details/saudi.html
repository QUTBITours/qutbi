Saudi Arabia Visa Requirements

Processing Time 5–10 working days
Validity Up to 90 days
Interview Required Yes

Types of Saudi Arabia Visas

Tourist Visa

For tourism purposes, including visiting family and friends.

Single or multiple entry, valid for up to 90 days.

Business Visa

For business purposes, including attending meetings, conferences, and negotiations.

Single or multiple entry, valid for up to 90 days.

Hajj or Umrah Visa

For pilgrims attending Hajj or Umrah.

Specific entry dates, usually valid for a short duration around pilgrimage dates.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid for at least 6 months from your intended date of arrival in Saudi Arabia with at least 2 blank pages.

Photograph

Two recent passport-sized photographs (white background, 35x45mm).

Flight Tickets

Confirmed round-trip flight booking.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed hotel accommodation in Saudi Arabia for the duration of your stay.

Bank Statement

Last 3 months bank statement showing sufficient funds for the duration of the trip.

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Choose Visa Type

Select the appropriate visa type (Tourist, Business, Hajj/Umrah) based on your purpose of travel.

Step 2

Submit Documents

Upload your documents online or submit them through our agency for processing.

Step 3

Wait for Approval

Processing time usually takes 5-10 working days. You’ll be notified by email regarding approval.

Step 4

Visa Approval

Once approved, collect your visa or eVisa and make arrangements for your travel to Saudi Arabia.

Important Guidelines

Entry Conditions

Ensure your visa type matches your entry purpose and adhere to the rules outlined by Saudi immigration.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is highly recommended for health and emergency coverage while in Saudi Arabia.

Overstay Penalties

Overstaying your visa in Saudi Arabia can result in fines, deportation, or future travel bans.

Visa Validity

Ensure your visa remains valid for the entire duration of your stay and departure from Saudi Arabia.
