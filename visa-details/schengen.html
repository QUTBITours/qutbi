Schengen Visa Requirements

Processing Time 10-15 working days
Validity Up to 90 days
Embassy Fee Starts from ₹7,200

Types of Schengen Visas

Tourist Visa

Fee: ₹8,500

Valid for up to 90 days within a 180-day period.

Allows travel to all 27 Schengen countries.

Business Visa

Fee: ₹9,200

Issued for short-term business visits, meetings, or events.

Requires business invitation letter.

Required Documents

Passport

Original passport with at least 6 months validity and 2 blank pages.

Photographs

2 recent passport-size photos, 35x45mm, white background.

Cover Letter

Explaining purpose of travel, itinerary, and accommodation details.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed accommodation for entire stay duration.

Flight Reservation

Return flight tickets or itinerary showing entry and exit dates.

Bank Statement

Past 6 months’ bank statements with sufficient funds.

Travel Insurance

Minimum coverage of €30,000 valid in all Schengen countries.

Visa Processing Timeline

Day 1

Consultation & Document Collection

We collect required documents and guide you through the process.

Day 2-4

VFS Appointment Booking

We book your biometric and visa submission slot.

Day 5-14

Embassy Review

Your documents are verified and processed by the embassy.

Day 15

Visa Issued

Your visa is stamped and ready for travel!

Note: Timeline may vary depending on embassy load and holidays.

Important Guidelines

Biometric Required

Applicants must be present for biometric submission at VFS Global.

Embassy Selection

Visa must be applied through the country of main stay or first entry.

Rejection Policy

If rejected, a fresh application may be submitted after addressing concerns.

Visa Extensions

Visa cannot be extended from within the Schengen area.
