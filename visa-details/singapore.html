Singapore Visa Requirements

Processing Time 5-7 working days
Validity 30 days (Single/Multiple Entry)
Embassy Fee Starts from ₹2,700

Types of Singapore Visas

Tourist Visa

Fee: ₹3,200

Valid for 30 days with single or multiple entries.

Ideal for tourism, family visits, or leisure travel.

Business Visa

Fee: ₹3,500

For attending meetings, conferences, or business-related activities.

Valid for 30 days with multiple entry allowed.

Required Documents

Passport

Original passport with minimum 6 months validity from travel date.

At least 2 blank pages for visa stamping.

Photographs

2 recent color passport-size photographs (35mm x 45mm).

White background with 80% face visibility.

Flight Tickets

Confirmed return flight tickets showing travel dates.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed accommodation for entire stay duration.

Bank Statement

Recent 3 months’ bank statement showing adequate funds.

Stamped and signed by the bank.

Visa Application Form

Filled and signed Singapore visa application form (Form 14A).

Visa Processing Timeline

Day 1

Document Submission

Submit completed application with supporting documents.

Day 2-3

Application Review

Documents are verified and submitted to the embassy.

Day 4-6

Embassy Processing

Visa is processed by Singapore immigration department.

Day 7

Visa Delivery

Visa is issued and delivered to applicant.

Note: Timelines are indicative and may vary based on case complexity or holidays.

Important Guidelines

Entry Conditions

Entry to Singapore is subject to immigration approval even with a valid visa.

Visa Validity

Visa is valid for 30 days with an entry window of up to 60 days from date of issue.

Visa Extension

Extensions are not permitted under the tourist visa. A new visa must be applied for after exit.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is highly recommended for medical and travel emergencies.

COVID-19 Guidelines

Please follow the latest health protocols mandated by Singapore Immigration and ICA.
