Types of South Africa Visas

Tourist Visa

For tourism purposes, including visiting family and friends.

Single entry, valid for up to 90 days.

Business Visa

For business-related visits, such as attending conferences, meetings, and negotiations.

Single or multiple entry, valid for up to 90 days.

Study Visa

For individuals planning to study in South Africa.

Validity depends on the duration of the course or study program.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid for at least 6 months with at least two blank pages.

Photograph

Two recent passport-sized photographs (white background, 35x45mm).

Flight Tickets

Confirmed round-trip flight booking.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed hotel accommodation in South Africa for the duration of your stay.

Bank Statement

Last 3 months bank statement showing sufficient funds for the duration of your stay.

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Choose Visa Type

Select the appropriate visa type (Tourist, Business, Study) based on your travel purpose.

Step 2

Submit Documents

Submit the required documents either online or through our agency for processing.

Step 3

Attend Visa Interview

You may be required to attend an interview at the South African consulate or embassy.

Step 4

Wait for Approval

Processing time typically takes 10-15 working days. You'll be notified of approval.

Step 5

Visa Collection

Once approved, collect your visa or eVisa and prepare for your travel.

Important Guidelines

Entry Conditions

Ensure your visa is appropriate for the purpose of your travel and that you meet all requirements.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is recommended for health and emergency coverage during your stay in South Africa.

Overstay Penalties

Overstaying your visa in South Africa can result in fines, deportation, or future travel bans.

Visa Validity

Ensure that your visa is valid for the entire duration of your stay and that you leave before it expires.
