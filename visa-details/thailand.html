Thailand Visa Requirements

Processing Time 4-6 working days
Validity 15 to 30 days
Entry Type Single Entry

Types of Thailand Visas

Tourist Visa

Ideal for leisure travel and vacation.

Stay duration: Up to 30 days.

May be extended once from within Thailand.

Visa on Arrival

Available for Indian citizens for stays up to 15 days.

Documents must be carried physically.

Longer stays require pre-approved tourist visa.

Required Documents

Passport

Original passport valid for at least 6 months with 2 blank pages.

Photograph

One recent passport-size photograph, white background, 35x45mm.

Flight Tickets

Return air ticket confirmation showing complete itinerary.

Hotel Booking

Confirmed hotel reservation for entire stay duration.

Important Guidelines

Visa on Arrival (VOA)

VOA is allowed for Indian citizens for stays up to 15 days. Ensure you carry passport, return tickets, hotel booking, and funds in cash.

Extension

You may apply for a 7-day extension in Thailand by visiting the immigration office.

Photograph Requirements

Improper photo size or quality can result in rejection. Follow embassy specifications.
