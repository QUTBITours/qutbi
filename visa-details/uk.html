UK Visa Requirements

Processing Time 3–6 weeks (standard)
Validity 6 months to 10 years
Interview Required No (in most cases)

Types of UK Visas

Standard Visitor Visa

For tourism, business meetings, short-term study, or medical treatment.

Valid for up to 6 months per visit.

Long-Term Visitor Visa

Multiple-entry visa valid for 2, 5, or 10 years.

Each visit limited to 6 months.

Transit Visa

For travelers transiting through the UK en route to another country.

Short stay permitted during layover.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid passport with at least 6 months validity and one blank page.

Photograph

One recent passport-sized color photograph as per UKVI guidelines.

Visa Application Form

Completed online application form (VAF1A) with confirmation page.

Visa Fee Payment Receipt

Proof of payment for the visa application fee.

Appointment Confirmation

Confirmation of your appointment at the Visa Application Centre (VAC).

Additional Supporting Documents

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements for the last 6 months.
  • Income tax returns or Form 16 for the last 2 years.
  • Salary slips or proof of income.

Travel Itinerary

  • Flight bookings and accommodation details.
  • Detailed travel plans within the UK.

Employment or Business Proof

  • Employment letter or business registration documents.
  • Leave approval letter from employer.

Invitation Letter (if applicable)

  • Letter from UK resident inviting you.
  • Copy of inviter's passport and visa/residency proof.
  • Address and contact details of the inviter.

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Complete Online Application

Fill out the visa application form on the official UK government website.

Step 2

Pay Visa Fee

Make the payment for the visa application fee online.

Step 3

Book Appointment

Schedule an appointment at the nearest Visa Application Centre (VAC).

Step 4

Attend Appointment

Visit the VAC to submit your biometric data and supporting documents.

Step 5

Wait for Decision

Processing time varies; standard processing takes 3–6 weeks.

Note: Processing times are indicative and may vary based on individual circumstances and application volume.

Important Guidelines

Travel Insurance

While not mandatory, it is highly recommended to have travel insurance covering medical emergencies and trip cancellations.

Proof of Funds

Ensure you have sufficient funds to cover your stay in the UK, including accommodation, travel, and daily expenses.

Return Assurance

Provide evidence of ties to India, such as employment, property, or family, to demonstrate your intent to return after your visit.

Accurate Documentation

Submit complete and truthful information; discrepancies can lead to visa refusal.
