USA Visa Requirements

Processing Time 15-30 working days
Validity Up to 10 years (multiple entry)
Interview Required Yes

Types of USA Visas

B1 Visa (Business)

For business meetings, conferences, and negotiations.

Not valid for employment or long-term study.

B2 Visa (Tourism)

For tourism, visiting family/friends, or medical treatment.

Allows multiple entries for short visits.

Combined B1/B2 Visa

Allows both business and tourism purposes in one visa.

Most commonly issued for Indian travelers.

Required Documents

Passport

Valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond intended stay.

All old passports, if available.

Photographs

2 recent color photographs (5x5 cm) with white background.

DS-160 Form Confirmation

Online filled non-immigrant visa application form.

Visa Fee Payment Receipt

Proof of fee payment for the US visa interview.

Interview Appointment Letter

Scheduled visa interview appointment confirmation at the VAC and Consulate.

Additional Supporting Documents

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • IT returns / Form 16 (last 2 years)
  • Proof of income / salary slips

Ties to Home Country

  • Employment documents or business proof
  • Property ownership documents (if any)
  • Family ties evidence

If Invited by US Resident

  • Invitation letter
  • Copy of invitee's passport and visa/residency proof
  • Address and contact details

Visa Application Process

Step 1

Complete DS-160 Form

Submit the online application form on the official US visa site.

Step 2

Pay Visa Fee

Make payment and save the receipt for appointment booking.

Step 3

Schedule Appointments

Book your biometric and visa interview appointments online.

Step 4

Attend VAC & Embassy Interview

Visit the Visa Application Center and attend the interview at the US Consulate.

Note: Final decision rests with the US Embassy. Processing time and approval are not guaranteed.

Important Guidelines

Intent to Return

You must prove strong ties to India and intent to return after your trip.

Visa Validity

Visa validity does not guarantee duration of stay; entry is determined at the port of entry by CBP.

Interview Preparation

Be prepared to answer questions about your purpose of travel, job, and family background.

Travel Insurance

Recommended for medical emergencies, especially for elderly travelers.
