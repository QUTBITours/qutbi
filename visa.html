Frequently Asked Questions

What types of visas do you process?

+

We process tourist visas, business visas, visit visas, and some special categories like medical visas and transit visas for most countries around the world.

How long does the visa process take?

+

Processing times vary by country, ranging from 3-5 working days for countries like Dubai to 30-45 days for countries like Canada. The exact processing time for each country is displayed on their respective pages.

Is visa approval guaranteed?

+

While we have a high success rate, visa approval ultimately depends on the embassy's decision. We ensure your application is properly prepared to maximize chances of approval, but cannot guarantee the outcome.

What happens if my visa is rejected?

+

In case of rejection, we provide consultation on the reasons and advise on the next steps, which may include reapplication with additional documents or choosing an alternative destination.

Do I need to visit your office for the visa process?

+

For most visa applications, you can initiate the process remotely. However, some visa types may require you to visit our office once for document verification or biometrics, depending on embassy requirements.
